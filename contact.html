Hestia's legacy

While she is said to preside over all hearths, domestic and communal, the prytaneion of Olympia is considered to have been a significant site for Hestia and her cult.

The Olympic Games torch relay, taking inspiration from the ancient tradition, always begins near these remains with a flame ignited by sunlight concentrated through a parabolic mirror.

Hestia in the modern world

It is unknown if any of Hestia's eternal flames from antiquity continue to this day, but a small scattering of modern practitioners of Hellenism still honor her in their own homes.

We are building a map of places Hestia is still recognized around the world. Are you a practicing Hellenist with Hestia as part of your praxis? If so, connect with us about your ritual and we'll feature it in one of our upcoming articles!
